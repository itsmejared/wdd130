Barranco in Lima

Barranco is a bohemian district in Lima, Peru, known for its colorful murals, colonial architecture, and vibrant art scene. It features charming streets, the famous Puente de los Suspiros, and a lively nightlife, making it a favorite spot for locals and visitors alike.
